History

Let Us Know About LRT With Watch The Video !!!

In 2010, FastKl was successfully established as Malaysia's main metro system company, with John and the Malaysian Ministry of Transport working together to reach a consensus to make it more accessible to the public. There are five major metro lines under FastKl, all of which are located in major city centre areas. We also have up to 3 lines under construction.


Fastkl's first metro line was built in 2003 and has gone from being unheard of to being used by everyone. The line leads to the city centre, so many visitors and employees take the metro to the city centre for fun and work.


Over the next few years, the company has successfully built five major lines in cooperation with the transport authorities. These routes also lead to the main areas, and they also lead from remote areas to busy areas. This makes it possible for people who need to travel from remote locations to work or school in the city centre to enjoy a convenient service. In addition to this, it also boosts the economy around the metro routes.


The aim of Fastkl is to get people to their destinations in the fastest and most cost-effective way. In addition, people can avoid traffic jams and late arrivals by reducing the need to drive to their destinations. People can plan their daily routes and buy the company's value-for-money packages to get to and from their destinations every day.
