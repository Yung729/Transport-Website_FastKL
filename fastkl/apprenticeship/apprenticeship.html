Apprenticeship

Have just finish school or college but still stumped on what to do in the future?
How about joining our apprenticeship programs? You just might be the person we are looking for. Carve your future career with us at FastKL.

Available Programs

PROFESSIONAL METRO TRAIN DRIVER

Program Details :

The Metro Operations & Engineering Training course is designed to provide students a thorough understanding of metro rail systems. Participants will get certification from PACE and/or the Department of Skills Development, Ministry of Human Resource Malaysia, upon satisfactory completion. These would improve the workforce's competence and proficiency in running their system.

Programme Objective :

  • To build participants' train operation knowledge and abilities.
  • Participants will get the opportunity to operate the Signaling and Communication Based Train Control (CBTC) system while operating a train.
  • Actual train driving, fault-finding, and emergency train operating procedures are all part of this on-the-job training.
  • Participants will be able to log in their actual hands-on time actually operating the train.

PROFESSIONAL METRO TRAIN CONTROLLER

Program Details :

The Metro Operations & Engineering Training course is designed to provide students a thorough understanding of metro rail systems. Participants will get certification from PACE and/or the Department of Skills Development, Ministry of Human Resource Malaysia, upon satisfactory completion. These would improve the workforce's competence and proficiency in running their system.

Programme Objective :

  • To build participants' understanding of and proficiency with operation control centres.
  • Participants will get a special On the Job Mentoring programme at OCC during peak and off-peak times, including
  • Handling various circumstances and system recovery.
  • The operation control centre will be available for participants to clock-in on their real practical experience.

PROFESSIONAL METRO STATION OPERATION

Program Details :

The Metro Operations & Engineering Training course is designed to provide students a thorough understanding of metro rail systems. Participants will get certification from PACE and/or the Department of Skills Development, Ministry of Human Resource Malaysia, upon satisfactory completion. These would improve the workforce's competence and proficiency in running their system.

Programme Objective :

  • For the purpose of allowing participants to log their actual practical experience at stations.

What is FastKL apperentice programs?


Every year, FastKL take on new graduates that is eligible to join our own academy where we teach students on the know how of train transportation industry. This programs can differ from short courses of 6-months or up to 2-years in durations. Once our students graduates, they will get certificated by MQA and be eligible to join our workforce for the respective positions.
