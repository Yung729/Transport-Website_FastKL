Easy and safe Back-to-School !!!

Introduction

Students all throughout the nation will begin the new school year on March 20.Some students who have difficulty getting to school can take advantage of this opportunity to get to school quickly and more efficiently. As a result, from March 20 through May 5, students will be able to ride the Light Rail Transit for free. With this offer, students will be able to travel to their schools conveniently and affordably because our metro lines serve a large portion of the school district.

Terms and conditions

  • Students will only need to scan their student card or ID card to get a free ride on the lrt.
  • There is just one free roundtrip per day. You must start charging if you plan to make more than one round journey.
  • Parents of students can accompany their sons on with 40% discount
